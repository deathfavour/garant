GDPR Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2024

1. Overview

Garant Marine Group LLC is committed to compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (EU) 2016/679 where applicable. This policy outlines how we handle the personal data of individuals located in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the United Kingdom.

2. Data Controller

Garant Marine Group LLC acts as the Data Controller for personal data collected through this website. Contact:
Email: info@garantmarinegroup.com
Address: Varna, Bulgaria, UAE

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent — when you voluntarily submit your CV or contact form
  • Legitimate interests — to operate and improve our recruitment services
  • Legal obligation — where required by applicable law
  • Contract performance — to fulfil crew placement agreements

4. Data We Collect

  • Identity data: name, date of birth, nationality
  • Contact data: email address, phone number
  • Professional data: rank, position, experience, certificates
  • Documents: CV files uploaded by applicants
  • Technical data: IP address, browser information (cookies)

5. Your GDPR Rights

If you are an EEA or UK resident, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access — request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification — correct inaccurate personal data
  • Right to Erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restriction — limit how we process your data
  • Right to Data Portability — receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to Object — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing

To exercise any right, email us at: info@garantmarinegroup.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. International Data Transfers

As a UAE-based company, your data may be processed outside the EEA. Where we transfer data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place in accordance with GDPR requirements, including standard contractual clauses where applicable.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected, or as required by law. Application data and CVs are retained for a maximum of 24 months. You may request earlier deletion at any time.

8. Complaints

If you believe your data protection rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority. EEA residents may contact their national Data Protection Authority.
