Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2024

1. Introduction

Garant Marine Group LLC ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose and safeguard your information when you visit our website GarantMarineGroup.com or submit information through our forms.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Professional details: rank, position, nationality, date of birth
  • CV and related documents you upload voluntarily
  • Company name and fleet information (for shipowner inquiries)
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, pages visited (via cookies)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process and respond to your job applications or business inquiries
  • Match seafarers with suitable vessel opportunities
  • Communicate with you regarding your application or inquiry status
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Shipowners and vessel operators solely for the purpose of crew placement (with your consent)
  • Service providers who assist in website hosting and email delivery
  • Regulatory authorities where required by law

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by applicable law. CV files and application data are typically retained for 24 months unless you request earlier deletion.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: info@garantmarinegroup.com

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure or destruction. Our website uses SSL/TLS encryption (HTTPS) and is protected via Cloudflare.

8. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience. Please see our Cookie Policy for full details.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:
