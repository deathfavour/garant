Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2024

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, analyse traffic and improve functionality. Cookies cannot execute programs or deliver viruses.

2. How We Use Cookies

GarantMarineGroup.com uses cookies to:

  • Ensure the website functions correctly
  • Analyse how visitors use our website (anonymous statistics)
  • Remember your preferences during a session
  • Protect the site via Cloudflare security services

3. Types of Cookies We Use

TypePurposeDuration
Essential Required for the website to function. Cannot be disabled. Session
Analytics Help us understand how visitors interact with the site (anonymous data). Up to 2 years
Security Set by Cloudflare to protect against bots and malicious traffic. Session / 1 year
Functional Remember user preferences such as language or form state. Session

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies, including:

  • Cloudflare — security and performance protection
  • Google Maps — embedded map on the Contact page

These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies, which we encourage you to review.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of this website. Instructions for managing cookies in common browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

6. Your Consent

By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. You may withdraw consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings or contacting us directly.

7. Contact

For questions about our cookie use, contact: info@garantmarinegroup.com
